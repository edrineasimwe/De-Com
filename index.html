FAQs

What is De-Com?

De-Com is a decentralized e-commerce platform built on the Stellar blockchain.

How do I connect my wallet?

You can use supported wallets like Freighter to securely connect and transact.

Yes, except for a minimal Stellar network fee (~0.00001 XLM per transaction).
